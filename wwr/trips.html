The Canyon Explorer (3 Days)

An intermediate trip down the scenic Red Rock Canyon. This multi-day adventure includes riverside camping, guided hiking, and moderate Class III rapids. Ideal for families and intermediate paddlers looking for a challenge.

  • **River**: Grand Canyon tributary
  • **Difficulty**: Class III
  • **Duration**: 3 Days / 2 Nights
  • **Starting Price**: $650 per person

Family Float Fun (Half-Day)

Perfect for beginners and young children, this relaxing half-day float focuses on the beautiful views and gentle Class I-II rapids. A great introduction to rafting with minimal splash and maximum safety.

  • **River**: Upper Willow Creek
  • **Difficulty**: Class I-II (Easy)
  • **Duration**: 4 Hours
  • **Starting Price**: $95 per person

The Adrenaline Rush (Full-Day)

For the experienced thrill-seeker! This full-day run tackles challenging Class IV and V rapids that demand teamwork and physical endurance. Prior rafting experience is highly recommended.

  • **River**: Lower Devil's Gorge
  • **Difficulty**: Class IV-V (Expert)
  • **Duration**: 7 Hours
  • **Starting Price**: $220 per person
